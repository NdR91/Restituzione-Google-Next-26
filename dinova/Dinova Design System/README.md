## Company Overview

**Dinova** (dinova.one) is a Bologna-based Italian digital innovation partner that guides companies through technological transformation. Their tagline is _"Innovation does. Intelligence guides."_

They operate through **four Competence Centers**:
1. **AI & Data Solutions** — custom software, system integration, process automation, intelligent agents, data engineering
2. **Cybersecurity** — cyber risk & governance, compliance & awareness, cyber defence & intelligence, secure automation, human factor
3. **Brand & Digital** — brand strategy & communication, service & product design, data & intelligent analytics, performance & data-driven marketing, digital transformation, digital governance
4. **Cloud** — cloud engineering, managed infrastructure, hybrid & multicloud, cloud security, AI-ready platforms

**Proprietary products**: LUCA (Human AI Workplace), Deep Academy (AI learning), Deep Scan (Human Risk Profiling), XYLER (Cyber Threat Detection & Response), Cyber Ninja (Cybersecurity Awareness)

**Key numbers**: +1600 clients, +8300 projects, +350 people

**Address**: Via dei Lapidari 12, 40129 Bologna, Italia | T. +39 051 6120291 | E. info@dinova.one

**Client roster**: Ducati, Maserati, Lavazza, Michelin, Coin, Comune di Bologna, Smartsheet, Fortinet, and many more.

**Three intervention modes**:
- **Consolidate** — secure and optimize existing processes, identity, systems
- **Enhance** — activate intelligence in processes, innovation culture
- **Enable** — enable full AI adoption & autonomous innovation

## CONTENT FUNDAMENTALS

### Tone & Voice
- **Language**: Italian primary (company profile and guidelines are in Italian); English used for headers, taglines, product names, and international-facing sections
- **Register**: Professional but human — warm, confident, forward-looking. Not stiff corporate speak.
- **POV**: Third-person for company description ("Dinova accompagna…"), second-person ("ti affianchiamo") in client-facing copy
- **Vibe**: Strategic, innovative, trustworthy. Positions itself as a *partner* not a vendor. Emphasis on *human* intelligence alongside AI.
- **Capitalization**: Italian sentence case. Product names in ALL CAPS or Title Case (LUCA, XYLER, DEEP ACADEMY). Section headers often in CAPS.
- **Emoji**: None used anywhere in official materials.
- **Numbers**: Bold stats used prominently ("+1600 Clienti", "+8300 Progetti", "+350 Persone")
- **Key phrases**: "intelligenza umana e artificiale", "singolarità delle persone", "valore concreto", "approccio modulare e flessibile", "data-driven", "cyber-resiliente", "cloud-native"
- **Punctuation**: Italian style; em dash used rarely; periods after section labels (e.g. "Consolidate." as label)
- **Brand name**: Always "Dinova" — never "DINOVA" in body copy, though "DINOVA" appears as a header/label in slide chrome

### Copy Examples
- Vision: _"Creare l'ecosistema dove il miglioramento delle persone stimola l'evoluzione continua delle organizzazioni."_
- Mission: _"Alimentiamo la singolarità delle persone e amplifichiamo la conoscenza con la tecnologia per anticipare i bisogni dei nostri clienti e fornire soluzioni agili e sostenibili."_
- Tagline: _"Innovation does. Intelligence guides."_ (English)
- AI slide: _"AI. Now is the Time."_

## VISUAL FOUNDATIONS

### Colors
**Primary palette** (from theme "Zerodiciotto"):
- `#00121A` — Dark Navy / Near-black (primary dark background)
- `#873BFF` — Violet / Brand Purple (primary brand color, logo, CTAs, links)
- `#FFFFFF` — White (text on dark backgrounds)
- `#F0F4F7` — Off-white / Light gray (light surface)

**Secondary / Accent palette**:
- `#FF7031` — Orange (accent 1, warm highlight)
- `#FF70F3` — Hot Pink / Magenta (accent 2, vibrant pop)
- `#5CD9FF` — Cyan / Electric Blue (accent 3, tech highlight)
- `#AAE505` — Lime Green (accent 4, data/success highlight)
- `#49556D` — Slate Gray (accent 5, muted secondary)
- `#A7BBD8` — Light Blue-Gray (accent 6, subtle)

**Additional tones seen in slides**:
- `#6B3DFF` — Brighter violet (slide background variation)
- `#7B2FFF` — Mid violet
- `#00335B` — Deep navy (visited links)

### Typography
The brand uses **Poppins** as the primary typeface (multiple weights embedded: ExtraLight, Light, Regular, Medium, SemiBold, Bold, ExtraBold) and **Rubik** appears in the template (ExtraBold). **Inter** is also embedded but used for supporting text. **Gill Sans** and **Helvetica Neue** appear in the theme but are fallbacks.

- **Headlines / Display**: Poppins SemiBold or ExtraBold — large, impactful
- **Body**: Poppins Regular or Light — clean, readable
- **Labels / Captions**: Poppins Medium, often spaced/uppercase
- **Accent type**: Sometimes Rubik ExtraBold for impact statements

**Type scale used in slides** (16:9 at 33.87cm wide ≈ 1270px):
- Cover title: ~60–80pt, Poppins SemiBold/ExtraBold
- Section header: ~40–48pt
- Body text: ~18–24pt
- Captions/labels: ~12–14pt

### Backgrounds & Surfaces
- **Primary dark**: Near-black `#00121A` with the Dinova "O" logo mark as a large geometric element in violet — this is the signature slide background
- **Violet solid**: Full-bleed `#873BFF` violet used for chapter openers
- **Violet gradient**: Radial/soft gradient from bright violet to dark — used in cover slides
- **White/light**: `#FFFFFF` or `#F0F4F7` for content slides
- **Photographic**: Real photos with heavy violet color overlay (duotone violet effect) used for chapter openers and covers
- **Abstract tech backgrounds**: Dark navy with swirling purple particle lines (data visualization aesthetic), 3D wireframe city grids in purple

### Illustrations
Custom **flat-design modern** illustrations featuring:
- Stylized characters in purple clothing with bold black outlines
- Vibrant accent colors: cyan, hot pink, lime green, orange floating around characters
- Characters depicted as professionals interacting with tech (VR headsets, design tools, AI interfaces)
- Available in both "positivo" (white/light BG) and "negativo" (dark BG) variants
- NO gradients, NO shadows — flat fills only in illustrations

### Infographics / Diagrams
- Use primary and secondary palette in free combinations
- Must remain **simple and linear** — no gradients or sfumature
- Prefer continuous thin lines (1–2px) over dashed lines
- Geometric shapes: circles, rectangles — minimal and clean

### The "O" Brand Mark
The standout visual element is the **Dinova "O"** — a bold circular letter form (the "o" in "dinova.one") used as a giant background graphic. It appears:
- At massive scale as a slide background element
- In multiple colorways: violet on dark, dark violet on white, purple on purple gradient
- Used as a compositional device — often cropped, bleeding off the edge

### Iconography
Custom **outline icon set** ("icone") in two variants:
- **Positivo**: Dark outlines on light background
- **Negativo**: Light outlines on dark background
- Style: outline stroke, selective violet accent on key details, minimal, direct
- No emoji used. No unicode characters used as icons.
- Icons organized by category

### Corner Radii
- Cards/containers: small radius (~4–8px), not pill-shaped
- Buttons: slightly rounded (~6px)
- Large decorative elements: organic curves (the "O" shape)

### Cards
- Light cards: white background, very subtle border or shadow, slight rounding
- Dark cards: `#00121A` or dark navy background, white text, no border
- No heavy drop shadows — flat or very subtle elevation

### Shadows
Minimal shadow use — brand prefers flat or very subtle depth cues. No complex inner/outer shadow systems.

### Animations / Motion
No documented animation system. Presentations are static. Transitions should be:
- Clean, functional fades or wipes
- No bouncy or playful physics — professional and measured

### Hover / Press States
Not documented in slide context. For digital, expected:
- Hover: slight opacity reduction (0.85) or subtle background darkening
- Press/active: slight scale-down or color deepening
- No dramatic effects

### Use of Transparency / Blur
- Photo overlays use flat violet duotone (not blur)
- Occasional semi-transparent surfaces for text legibility on photo backgrounds

### Imagery Vibe
- Photos: warm, luminous, slightly desaturated — natural poses, positive attitudes, depth-of-field blur in background
- After purple overlay: cool-tinted, atmospheric
- No stock-photo aesthetic — candid, human moments

## ICONOGRAPHY

Dinova uses a **custom icon set** with:
- **Style**: Outline / stroke icons, ~2px weight, selective violet accent (#873BFF) on a detail per icon
- **Variants**: "Positivo" (dark on light) and "Negativo" (light/white on dark)
- **Categories**: Referenced in slides as organized by category (covers tech, security, cloud, data, etc.)
- **Format**: PNG files (mentioned: "icone positivo" and "icone negativo" folders)
- **No CDN icon library** referenced — fully custom set

> ⚠️ The actual icon PNG files from the custom set were not individually extractable from the PPTX (they appear embedded in slide layouts, not as standalone media). The slides reference separate "folder con Icone" assets. **Ask the Dinova team for the icon PNG/SVG folder to include here.**

For production use, the closest CDN substitute matching the style would be **Phosphor Icons** (outline, clean, 2px stroke weight): `https://unpkg.com/phosphor-icons`

The logo "O" mark appears in these variants (available in `assets/`):
- `assets/logo-o-violet.png` — bright violet on white
- `assets/logo-o-dark.png` — dark navy on white  
- `assets/logo-o-black.png` — near-black on white
- `assets/logo-o-violet-alt.png` — violet, slightly different crop
- `assets/logo-o-stacked.png` — stacked double-O (logo mark variant)
- `assets/bg-o-dark.png` — the O as large dark BG element

The full official **wordmark** is available in two variants:
- `assets/logo-wordmark-dark.png` — "dinova" wordmark in dark navy (#00121A) with violet O — use on white/light backgrounds
- `assets/logo-wordmark-white.png` — "dinova" wordmark in white with violet O — use on dark or violet backgrounds

The wordmark uses a custom typeface (not Poppins) with the distinctive violet "O" as the letter "o" in "dinova".
